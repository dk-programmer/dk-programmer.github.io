Who am I?

I'm a computer science student in Berlin with the goal to create games and worlds with Unity3D. The joy of creating games started more than six years ago with some small prototype games. After a lot of self education and some bigger projects I can call myself a gamedev. I've learned a lot of things at game conferences and had a great time presenting our games at the Tokyo Game Show and Game Daily Connect. I'm currently working on a voice controlled horror game.
