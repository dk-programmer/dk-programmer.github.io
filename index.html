Who am I?

I'm a computer science student living in Berlin, specialized in creating games and worlds in Unity3D. The joy of creating games started more than six years ago with some small prototype games. After a period of self education and leading two indie game projects, I entered the gaming industry. My current project 'The Voice Inside' is a voice controllable horror-adventure game.
